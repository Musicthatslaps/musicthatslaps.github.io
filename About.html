ABOUT


MusicThatSlaps was a concept developed to appeal to hip hop enthusiasts who feel left out in the music conversation. We are on a mission to find that next song that will make you experience that beautiful eargasm.
This website celebrates those in the main stream as well as those in the more niche categories, so you will be able to find a great variety of music within our genres. Use MusicThatSlaps as the launchpoint to your musical library
and social circle if you're interested in meeting like-minded individuals who eat, sleeps, and breathes music.
The website is designed for community and to allow conversation about the music we love to flourish and help grow the knowledge of music for the members who make this website what it is. May this website provide a space
for you to grow as an individual who loves music and to appreciate all of the beauty that comes with the process of making something that appeals to the masses.
